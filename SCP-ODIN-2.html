Special Containment Procedures
SCP-ODIN is to be kept in a standard home with its owners. The house requires basic furnishings for SCP-ODIN and its handler - bedding, food, water, etc. SCP-ODIN and its handlers are permitted to roam outside the house with supervision. Unless there are any sightings of dogs or cats in the area. The subject has shown extreme aggression toward said animals.
Personnel who come in contact with SCP-ODIN are not permitted to show things that produce air, like blowing your mouth, air pumps, and so on: SCP-ODIN is extremely hostile to such items. with the exception of electric fans and air conditioning. For the same reason, personnel who come in contact with SCP-ODIN are not permitted to physically fight with anyone in sight of the SCP, this also includes the handlers of SCP-ODIN.

SCP-ODIN.
Description:
SCP-ODIN presents as a male dog of the Lhasa apso breed: based on analysis, the subject's age is approximately five (5) years old or 36 years old in human years. The subject appears to be physically normal beyond five (5) significant abilities that make the subject an anomaly:
1. The subject can produce barks louder than a gunshot. This doesn't affect the handler of SCP-ODIN.
2. The subject can change its speed based on the situation, the fastest SCP-ODIN has been seen is about 80 - 130 km/h.
3. The subject's bite has a paralyzing effect like a snake that can last up to 15-40 minutes. This too doesn't affect the handler.
4. The ‚Äúlick‚Äù of the subject has a healing effect on wounds, broken bones, and so on. We still don't know how this happens.
5. The subject has a razor-sharp teeth that can pierce any clothing and armor.
Note* All of these abilities can be amplified when the subject is angry, scared, sad, or hungry.
Those are the 5 recorded abilities of SCP-ODIN. (Some of the information about SCP-ODIN are redacted) [Learn more about the abilities of SCP-ODIN]
