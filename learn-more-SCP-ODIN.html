[Start Log]
Hello, my name is Dr. Wesley Mcelravy, my team and I have discovered that SCP-Odin has a secret ability.
The secret ability of SCP-ODIN is that it can turn into a god-like form. And while in “God-form” SCP-ODIN can achieve immortality, super strength, super speed, super jump, super eyesight, poison-resistants, acid resistance, fire resistance, explosion resistance, cold resistance, can fly, can turn invisible, some instances can pass thru solid object. That is all we know as of now. We are still studying SCP-ODIN.
We know this because of an [Incident] while transporting SCP-ODIN to the facility. The subject got scared and angry thus attacking the taskforce. Report shows that they all Died..
SCP-ODIN is dangerous to everyone.
[End Log]
